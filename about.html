Our Journey

A Store Built on Trust

Lalit Electronics was founded with a single purpose: to give the people of Bhawanipatna access to genuine, high-quality electronics at fair prices — backed by honest advice and real after-sale care.

Located on the heart of Townhall Road, near Sai Mandir, our showroom has grown from a small electronics outlet to one of the most recognised names in Kalahandi district. We stock products from India's leading brands including Samsung, LG, Sony, Whirlpool, Godrej, and many more.

ଆମ ଲକ୍ଷ୍ୟ ହେଲା ଭବାନୀପଟ୍ଟଣାର ଲୋକଙ୍କୁ ଉତ୍ତମ ଇଲେକ୍ଟ୍ରୋନିକ୍ସ ସଞ୍ଚୟ ମୂଲ୍ୟରେ ଦେବା।

We believe that buying an appliance should be a joyful experience — not a stressful one. That's why our staff takes time to understand your needs, budget, and space before recommending any product. We never push a sale; we guide a decision.

Our Story Over the Years

From humble beginnings to Bhawanipatna's most trusted name in electronics — here's how we've grown.

2021
Grand Opening

Lalit Electronics opened its doors on Townhall Road with a curated selection of TVs, refrigerators and mobile phones.

2022
Expanded to Appliances & Furniture

Customer demand led us to add a full range of home appliances and furniture, becoming a one-stop shop for every home need.

2023
Free Delivery Program Launched

We introduced free home delivery within Bhawanipatna town, bringing our products straight to our customers' doorsteps.

2024
100+ Customer Reviews Milestone

Crossed 100 five-star Google reviews — a testament to the trust and satisfaction of our loyal community.

2025–Present
4.8★ Rating & Growing

150+ happy reviews, a 4.8-star Google rating, and a growing family of satisfied customers across Bhawanipatna and Kalahandi district.
